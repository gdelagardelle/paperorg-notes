Privacy Policy

Paperorg Notes · Last updated: 9 July 2026

Paperorg Notes (“the App”) is a voice recording and transcription app for iOS, operated by Germain Delagardelle (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data the App processes, where it is stored, and what choices you have. We designed the App with privacy and GDPR compliance in mind.

1. Summary

  • Audio and transcripts are stored on your device by default.
  • Transcription may send audio to third-party services you configure (OpenAI, ElevenLabs, LuxASR).
  • We do not operate user accounts or cloud backends in the current version.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
  • We do not use your content to train AI models unless a provider's terms state otherwise and you have consented.

2. Data We Process

3. Third-Party Transcription Services

When you transcribe audio, the App may send audio files to external providers only after you have given explicit consent for that provider in Settings. You supply your own API keys (bring-your-own-key model).

ProviderData sentLocation
OpenAI Audio; transcript text for summarisation United States

LuxASR (University of Luxembourg) Audio for transcription Luxembourg / EU

Each provider's own privacy policy and data retention terms apply when you use their service. Review those policies before enabling a provider.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area and Luxembourg:

  • Consent — processing audio via third-party APIs, after you opt in per provider.
  • Contract / legitimate use — local recording, storage, and features you request on your device.

5. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights directly in the App (Settings → Privacy & GDPR):

  • Access — export all notes, transcripts, and audio (GDPR export).
  • Erasure — delete all data, including audio, transcripts, and API keys.
  • Rectification — edit transcripts; corrected text is stored separately from the original.
  • Portability — export as JSON, plain text, Markdown, or PDF.
  • Object / restrict — disable cloud providers; use on-device transcription where available.

You may also contact us at germain@delagardelle.lu to exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy.

6. Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • You may enable “Delete audio after transcription” or automatic deletion after a set number of days.
  • Third-party providers may retain data according to their own policies for the duration of API processing.

7. Security

  • API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Audio files use iOS Data Protection.
  • All network communication with transcription providers uses HTTPS (TLS).
  • Optional Face ID lock protects access to the App.

8. Children

The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
